1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how GameBoosterPro ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Device Information

  • System Performance Metrics: CPU usage, RAM availability, device temperature, and battery level (estimated/simulated values for optimization recommendations)
  • Device Specifications: Hardware capabilities, Android version, and device model
  • Network Information: Network latency and connection quality for gaming optimization

2.2 App Usage Data

  • User Preferences: Graphics settings, performance profiles, and optimization preferences
  • Usage Statistics: Number of optimizations performed, feature usage frequency, and session duration
  • Game Profiles: Custom graphics configurations for specific games (stored locally)

2.3 Permissions Data

  • System Access: Information about granted permissions (battery optimization, system settings modification, usage statistics access)
  • App Management: Information about installed applications for background process optimization

3. Permissions We Request

Battery Optimization Exemption

Prevents Android from killing the app during gaming sessions, ensuring consistent performance optimization.

Modify System Settings

Controls brightness, animations, and system performance settings for optimal gaming experience.

Usage Access

Monitors resource usage to identify and manage battery-draining apps that impact gaming performance.

Display Over Other Apps

Shows performance overlays and FPS counters over games for real-time monitoring.

Optional Permissions

Do Not Disturb Access

Automatically silences notifications during gaming sessions.

Phone State Access

Pauses games during incoming calls for seamless user experience.

WiFi Control

Optimizes network settings for reduced gaming latency.

Accessibility Service

Provides advanced system control and automation features.

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Core Functionality

  • Optimize device performance for gaming
  • Apply graphics settings and performance profiles
  • Monitor system resources and provide recommendations
  • Kill background processes that may impact gaming performance

4.2 Personalization

  • Remember your preferred settings and configurations
  • Provide AI-powered optimization recommendations based on device performance
  • Create and manage custom game profiles
  • Track optimization effectiveness and usage patterns

4.3 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality (data processed locally)
  • Identify which features are most effective for users
  • Optimize recommendation algorithms based on device performance data

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage Only

  • User Preferences: Stored locally using Android's SharedPreferences system
  • Game Profiles: Saved on your device using secure local storage
  • Performance History: Optimization statistics stored locally on your device

5.2 No Cloud Storage

  • We do not transmit your personal data to external servers
  • All user data remains on your device
  • No user accounts or online services are required

5.3 Data Security

  • All data is stored using Android's secure storage mechanisms
  • Settings are encrypted using platform-standard security measures
  • No sensitive personal information is collected or stored

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

6.1 No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data remains on your device and is not shared with external services.

6.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information only if required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process, when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, or investigate fraud.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Data Control

  • Access: All your data is accessible through the app's settings
  • Modification: You can modify or delete your preferences and profiles at any time
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device

7.2 Permission Management

  • You can revoke permissions at any time through Android Settings
  • The app will continue to function with limited capabilities if permissions are denied
  • Critical permissions are clearly explained before being requested

8. Children's Privacy

Parental guidance is recommended for users under 13. While our app does not collect personal information or share data with third parties, it does request advanced system permissions and modifies device settings. Parents should review these permissions and ensure they understand the app's functionality before allowing children to use it.

We recommend that parents:

  • Review the permissions requested by the app
  • Understand how the app modifies system settings
  • Monitor their child's device usage and gaming habits
  • Ensure the app is used as a tool to enhance gaming experience, not to encourage excessive screen time

9. Third-Party Services and Integrations

9.1 Android System Services

Our app interacts with various Android system services to provide optimization features:

  • ActivityManager: For managing background processes
  • PowerManager: For battery optimization features
  • NotificationManager: For Do Not Disturb functionality
  • Settings: For system configuration modifications

9.2 No Analytics or Advertising

We do not use third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking tools. Your usage data is not shared with external services for advertising or analytics purposes.

10. Data Retention

10.1 Retention Period

  • User preferences and game profiles are retained until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Performance statistics are stored locally and automatically cleaned up based on storage limitations
  • No data is retained after app uninstallation

10.2 Data Cleanup

The app automatically manages storage to prevent excessive data accumulation while preserving your important settings and profiles.
